Privacy

This is an academic project (NTU final-year dissertation), not a commercial platform. Plain-English summary of what we store and what your rights are.

What we store

  • Your username, email, hashed password, and optional bio.
  • Posts you write and comments you make, with timestamps.
  • Movies you've written about (metadata cached from the OMDb API).
  • Watch parties you create and RSVPs you make.

Where it's stored

A PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase. All connections to and from the database are encrypted in transit (TLS). Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed with BCrypt using a per-user salt, which means even the project author cannot read them.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party trackers.
  • No advertising of any kind.
  • No selling, sharing, or licensing of user data to anyone.
  • No cookies beyond a single session cookie that keeps you logged in.

Your rights (UK DPA 2018 & GDPR)

You can ask us to:

  • Send you a copy of all data associated with your account.
  • Delete your account and everything you've posted. The database cascades the deletion through your posts, comments, RSVPs and watch-party hosting; nothing personally identifying remains.
  • Withdraw consent for any user-testing data collection you may have agreed to.

Email the project author at N1205202@my.ntu.ac.uk to make any of these requests. Responses within 7 days.

Future work (and a limitation worth being honest about)

Account deletion is currently a manual process — we don't yet expose a self-service "delete my account" button in the UI. Flagged in the dissertation as future work for a production version.

Changes

Any material change to this policy will be communicated to active users by email before it takes effect.
