How to read this

Pool size: 100 recent posts. Feed size: 20. Pool median comments: 2.

  • Higher Shannon entropy = greater genre diversity. The diverse feed should be measurably higher than the baseline.
  • Higher unique-author ratio = more voices represented. Popularity-only ranking clusters around active users.
  • Higher long-tail share = more low-engagement posts surfaced. By design the diverse feed targets ~20% (the 1:5 quiet-pick ratio); baseline should be near 0%.
  • Lower mean comments per slot = quieter content reached. Confirms the quiet-pick slots actually fire.
